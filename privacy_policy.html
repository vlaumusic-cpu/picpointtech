PICPoint 隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026年1月12日

1. 引言

PICPoint（以下简称"本应用"）致力于保护用户的隐私和数据安全。本隐私政策旨在向您说明本应用的隐私保护措施，特别是本应用不收集、存储或传输任何用户个人数据的特性。

本应用仅在海外Apple Store发布，不包括中国区应用商店。

请您在使用本应用前仔细阅读本隐私政策。如果您不同意本政策的任何内容，请立即停止使用本应用。

2. 数据收集说明

本应用不收集、存储或传输任何用户个人数据，包括但不限于：

  • 您的照片或图片数据；
  • 您的个人身份信息；
  • 您的设备信息；
  • 您的使用行为数据。

3. 分析与处理方式

所有照片分析和心理分析过程均在您的设备本地完成，不会将任何数据发送到我们的服务器或第三方服务器：

  • 照片仅在您的设备内存中临时加载用于分析；
  • 分析结果仅存储在您的设备本地，由您完全控制；
  • 我们无法访问或查看您的照片、分析结果或任何个人信息。

4. 分享功能

如果您选择分享分析结果：

  • 分享过程由您的设备直接完成，我们不会参与或存储分享内容；
  • 分享内容的安全性和隐私性由您选择的分享平台负责；
  • 请在分享前仔细考虑您分享的内容和对象。

5. 用户权利

由于本应用不收集任何用户数据，您无需担心数据泄露或滥用问题：

  • 您完全控制自己的照片和分析结果；
  • 您可以随时在设备上删除照片和分析结果；
  • 您无需提供任何个人信息即可使用本应用的全部功能。

6. 年龄限制

本应用仅面向18岁以上的成年人。由于本应用不收集任何用户数据，因此不会涉及未成年人数据的处理问题。

7. 数据共享

由于本应用不收集任何用户数据，因此不存在数据共享问题：

  • 我们不会将您的任何数据出售、出租或分享给第三方；
  • 我们没有任何用户数据可用于共享或披露；
  • 任何法律程序要求我们提供用户数据时，我们无法提供任何信息，因为我们根本不收集数据。

8. 政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。当我们更新政策时，我们会修改页面顶部的"最后更新日期"，并在必要时通过应用内通知或其他方式告知您。

请定期查看本隐私政策以了解任何更改。继续使用本应用即表示您接受更新后的隐私政策。

9. 联系方式

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

电子邮件：geogurugeo@qq.com

我们将在收到您的请求后15个工作日内回复。

10. 免责声明

本应用提供的心理分析结果仅供参考，不构成任何医疗诊断或治疗建议。如有心理困扰，请咨询专业心理医生或医疗机构。

PICPoint Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Introduction

PICPoint ("the App") is committed to protecting user privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy explains the privacy protection measures of the App, particularly the fact that the App does not collect, store, or transmit any user personal data.

The App is only available on overseas Apple Stores and is not distributed in China.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please stop using the App immediately.

2. Data Collection Statement

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any user personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Your photos or image data;
  • Your personal identity information;
  • Your device information;
  • Your usage behavior data.

3. Analysis and Processing Methods

All photo analysis and psychological analysis processes are completed locally on your device and do not send any data to our servers or third-party servers:

  • Photos are only temporarily loaded in your device's memory for analysis;
  • Analysis results are only stored locally on your device and are fully controlled by you;
  • We cannot access or view your photos, analysis results, or any personal information.

4. Sharing Functionality

If you choose to share analysis results:

  • The sharing process is completed directly by your device; we do not participate in or store shared content;
  • The security and privacy of shared content are the responsibility of the sharing platform you choose;
  • Please carefully consider the content and recipients before sharing.

5. User Rights

Since the App does not collect any user data, you do not need to worry about data leakage or abuse:

  • You have full control over your photos and analysis results;
  • You can delete photos and analysis results from your device at any time;
  • You do not need to provide any personal information to use all features of the App.

6. Age Restriction

The App is only intended for adults over the age of 18. Since the App does not collect any user data, it does not involve the processing of minors' data.

7. Data Sharing

Since the App does not collect any user data, there is no data sharing issue:

  • We do not sell, rent, or share any of your data with third parties;
  • We do not have any user data available for sharing or disclosure;
  • When any legal procedure requires us to provide user data, we cannot provide any information because we do not collect data at all.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update the policy, we will modify the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page and, if necessary, inform you through in-app notifications or other means.

Please check this Privacy Policy regularly to understand any changes. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the following methods:

Email: geogurugeo@qq.com

We will respond within 15 working days after receiving your request.

10. Disclaimer

The psychological analysis results provided by the App are for reference only and do not constitute any medical diagnosis or treatment advice. If you have psychological distress, please consult a professional psychologist or medical institution.
