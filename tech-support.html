Technical Support

FAQ

  • What image formats are supported?
    PICpoint supports JPG, PNG, WEBP, and HEIC formats (maximum file size: 10MB).
  • How long does the analysis take?
    Analysis time depends on the image complexity, typically ranging from 5 to 30 seconds.
  • Is my data secure?
    Yes, PICpoint prioritizes your privacy. All image processing is performed securely without storing your images.
  • Why is the analysis report not displaying properly?
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.
  • How do I switch languages?
    The app automatically detects your device language. You can also change the language within the app settings.

Troubleshooting

  • App crashes on startup:
    Try updating to the latest version of iOS and PICpoint. If the issue continues, reinstall the app.
  • Image upload failed:
    Check your internet connection and ensure the image size is within the 10MB limit.
  • Analysis results are inaccurate:
    For best results, upload clear, high-resolution images with good lighting.
